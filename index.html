📋 Tara Air 9N-AET | Investigation Abstract

🕒 Accident Overview

On 29 May 2022, Tara Air flight 9N-AET crashed near Jomsom, Nepal. All 22 occupants lost their lives.

🔧 Key Findings

  • Captain hesitated but continued
  • TAWS intentionally inhibited
  • First Officer: 520 hrs vs Captain: 17,500 hrs
  • Poor Crew Resource Management
⚠️ Key Finding: Plan continuation bias, authority gradient, drift into failure.
